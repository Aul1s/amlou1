About Us

Who we are

We are a company based in Agadir, Morocco, dedicated to producing high-quality Amlou products. Our Amlou spreads are made from locally-sourced almonds, argan oil, and honey, and we are committed to using only the finest ingredients.

We are passionate about bringing the unique flavors of Morocco to the world and strive to share the health benefits of Amlou with our customers.
